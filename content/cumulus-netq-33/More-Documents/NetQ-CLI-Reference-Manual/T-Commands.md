This topic includes all commands that begin with `netq t*`, `netq u*`, `netq v*`, `netq w*`, `netq x*`, `netq y*`, and `netq z*`.

## netq trace

    <ip>   :  IPv4 or v6 address (no mask)
    <mac>  :  MAC address
